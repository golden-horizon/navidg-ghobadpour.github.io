About Me

I began my technical journey with Cisco networking, developing strong foundations in routing, switching, VLANs, subnetting and network security. I then expanded into cybersecurity focusing on governance, risk and compliance frameworks such as ISO 27001 and the Essential Eight maturity model.

My experience includes security assessments, vulnerability analysis, SIEM monitoring, firewall configuration, VPN deployment and secure infrastructure design.

I am always interested in contributing to projects related to cybersecurity, cloud security and secure application development.
